## ‚úÖ **Design Changes Applied**

### **1. Salesforce Color Scheme**
- **Primary Blue:** #0176D3 (Salesforce blue)
- **Text Primary:** #16325C (Dark blue-gray)
- **Text Secondary:** #3E3E3C (Medium gray)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (White)
- **Background Secondary:** #F3F2F2 (Light gray)
- **Border:** #D8DDE6 (Light border)
- **Error:** #C23934 (Red)

### **2. Typography**
- **Font Family:** "Salesforce Sans", Arial, sans-serif
- **Section Titles:** 13px, bold, uppercase
- **Labels:** 12px, regular weight
- **Inputs:** 13px, regular weight
- **Buttons:** 13px, regular weight

### **3. Form Layout**
- **Modal:** White background, rounded corners (4px)
- **Sections:** White cards with subtle shadows
- **Grid:** 2 columns (3 on large screens)
- **Spacing:** 16px gaps, consistent padding

### **4. Input Fields**
- **Border:** #DDDBDA (Light gray)
- **Focus:** Blue border (#0176D3) with shadow
- **Hover:** Darker border (#706E6B)
- **Height:** 32px minimum
- **Padding:** 8px 12px

### **5. Buttons**
- **Primary:** Blue (#0176D3) with white text
- **Secondary:** White with gray border
- **Hover:** Darker shades
- **Height:** 32px
- **Padding:** 8px 16px

### **6. Modal**
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Backdrop:** Dark blue-gray with blur
- **Shadow:** Subtle shadow for depth
- **Border Radius:** 4px
