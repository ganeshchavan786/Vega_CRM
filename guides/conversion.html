Overview

Conversion transforms a qualified lead into a permanent customer account with contacts and opportunities. The system uses an Account-First Model where accounts are created first, then contacts, then opportunities.

‚úÖ Conversion Eligibility

A lead is eligible for conversion when:

  • Lead Score > 70
  • Lead Status = "Contacted"

Conversion Process

The system automatically performs these steps:

1 Create Account (Customer)

Company name becomes account name. All account fields are populated from lead data.

2 Create Contact

Lead's name becomes contact. Contact is linked to account and marked as primary.

3 Create Opportunity (Deal)

Deal created with:

  • Deal value from budget range
  • Close date from timeline
  • Stage: Prospect (25% probability)
4 Log Activity

Conversion activity is automatically logged in timeline.

5 Update Lead Status

Lead status changes to "Converted" and is linked to the new account.

How to Convert a Lead

1 Check Eligibility

View lead details and check conversion eligibility. System shows if lead meets criteria.

2 Preview Conversion

Click "Preview Conversion" to see what will be created (Account, Contact, Deal details).

3 Convert Lead

Click "Convert Lead" button. System performs all steps automatically.

4 Review Results

After conversion, you'll see:

  • Created account ID and name
  • Created contact ID and name
  • Created deal ID and value
  • Activity log entry
