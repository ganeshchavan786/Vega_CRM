Overview

Customers (also called Accounts) are permanent entities in your CRM. They represent companies or organizations you do business with. Unlike leads, accounts are never deleted - they can only be marked as inactive.

Key Features

üè¢ Enterprise Fields

  • Account Type: Customer, Prospect, Partner, Competitor, Reseller
  • Industry: IT Services, Healthcare, Manufacturing, etc.
  • Company Size: Small (1-50), Medium (51-200), Large (200+)
  • Annual Revenue: Track yearly revenue
  • GSTIN: Indian tax identification number

üìä Health Score (Auto-Calculated)

The system automatically calculates health scores (0-100) based on:

  • Activity count (more activities = higher score)
  • Deal stages (active deals = higher score)
  • Recency (recent activities = higher score)
  • Account status

üîÑ Lifecycle Stage (Auto-Determined)

Automatically transitions between stages:

  • MQA: Marketing Qualified Account
  • SQA: Sales Qualified Account
  • Customer: Has won deals
  • Churned: Inactive for 90+ days

How to Create a Customer

1 Navigate to Customers Page

Click on "Customers" in the sidebar navigation menu.

2 Click "Add Customer" Button

You'll find the "Add Customer" button at the top right of the customers list page.

3 Fill in Customer Information

Complete the customer form with the following information:

  • Name: Company or customer name (required)
  • Email: Primary email address
  • Phone: Contact phone number
  • Account Type: Select from dropdown (Customer, Prospect, etc.)
  • Industry: Select or enter industry
  • Company Size: Select size category
  • Annual Revenue: Enter revenue amount
  • GSTIN: Enter GSTIN if applicable
  • Address: Complete address information
4 Assign Owner

Select the account owner who will be responsible for this customer.

5 Save Customer

Click "Save" or "Create" button. The system will:

  • Create the customer record
  • Calculate initial health score
  • Set initial lifecycle stage

1 Customer List

The customers page shows a table with all customers, including:

Editing Customers

1 Open Edit Form

Click the "Edit" button (pencil icon) next to the customer you want to edit.

2 Update Information

Modify any fields you want to update. The form will pre-fill with existing data.

3 Save Changes

Click "Save" to update the customer. Health score and lifecycle stage will be recalculated automatically.

Health Score Explained

The health score is automatically calculated and ranges from 0-100:

  • 0-30: Low health - Needs attention
  • 31-60: Medium health - Regular monitoring
  • 61-80: Good health - Active engagement
  • 81-100: Excellent health - Strong relationship

Factors affecting health score:

  • Number of activities (calls, emails, meetings)
  • Active deals in pipeline
  • Recency of last activity
  • Account status (active vs inactive)

Lifecycle Stages

The system automatically determines lifecycle stage based on:

  • MQA (Marketing Qualified Account): New account with initial engagement
  • SQA (Sales Qualified Account): Account with active deals
  • Customer: Account with won deals
  • Churned: Account inactive for 90+ days
