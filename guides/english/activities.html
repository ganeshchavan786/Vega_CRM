Overview

Activities log all interactions with customers, leads, and deals. They create a complete timeline of your engagement history.

üìà Auto Score Increment

When you log activities for leads, the lead score automatically increases based on activity type and engagement level.

Activity Types

  • Call: Phone call made or received
  • Email: Email sent or received
  • Meeting: In-person or virtual meeting
  • Note: General note or observation
  • Status Change: Status update

How to Log an Activity

1 Navigate to Activities

Click "Activities" in the sidebar or access from Customer/Lead/Deal detail page.

2 Click "Add Activity"

Click the "Add Activity" button.

3 Fill Activity Details
  • Activity Type: Call, Email, Meeting, Note
  • Title: Brief description
  • Description: Detailed notes
  • Duration: Time spent (minutes)
  • Outcome: Positive, Negative, Neutral, Follow-up Required
  • Related To: Link to Customer, Lead, Deal, or Task
4 Save Activity

Click "Save". The activity will appear in the timeline and may increase lead scores.

Timeline View

The timeline shows all activities chronologically, providing a complete history of interactions.

Tips

üí° Tip: Log activities immediately after interactions to maintain accurate records.
üí° Tip: Use outcomes to track engagement quality and identify follow-up needs.
