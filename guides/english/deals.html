Overview

Deals (also called Opportunities) represent potential revenue from customer accounts. Track deals through the sales pipeline from initial prospect to closed won or lost.

üìä Sales Pipeline

The CRM uses a 6-stage pipeline to track deals:

  1. Prospect (10%): Initial opportunity identified
  2. Qualified (25%): Opportunity qualified
  3. Proposal (50%): Proposal sent
  4. Negotiation (75%): In negotiation
  5. Closed Won (100%): Deal won
  6. Closed Lost (0%): Deal lost

How to Create a Deal

1 Navigate to Deals Page

Click on "Deals" in the sidebar navigation menu.

2 Click "Add Deal" Button

Click the "Add Deal" button at the top right of the deals page.

3 Select Customer Account

Required: Select the customer account this deal belongs to. Deals must be linked to an account.

4 Fill in Deal Information

Complete the deal form:

Basic Information:

  • Deal Name: Name of the opportunity (required)
  • Deal Value: Expected revenue amount (required)
  • Currency: INR, USD, etc.

Pipeline Information:

  • Stage: Current pipeline stage (Prospect, Qualified, Proposal, etc.)
  • Probability: Win probability percentage (0-100%)
  • Forecast Category: Best Case, Commit, Most Likely, Worst Case

Dates:

  • Expected Close Date: When you expect to close the deal
  • Actual Close Date: Actual close date (filled when won/lost)

Additional:

  • Primary Contact: Link to contact (optional)
  • Lead: Link to source lead (optional)
  • Notes: Additional information
  • Assigned To: Sales rep responsible
5 Save Deal

Click "Save" to create the deal. It will appear in your sales pipeline.

Pipeline Stages Explained

Stage Probability Description
Prospect 10% Initial opportunity identified, needs qualification
Qualified 25% Opportunity qualified, meets BANT criteria
Proposal 50% Proposal sent, waiting for response
Negotiation 75% In active negotiation, close to closing
Closed Won 100% Deal won, revenue realized
Closed Lost 0% Deal lost, record loss reason

Moving Deals Through Pipeline

1 Edit Deal

Click "Edit" on the deal you want to move.

2 Update Stage

Change the "Stage" field to the next stage in your pipeline.

3 Update Probability

The probability will automatically update based on stage, but you can manually adjust it.

4 Save Changes

Save to update the deal stage. The deal will move in your pipeline view.

Closing Deals

Winning a Deal:

1 Update Status

Change deal status to "Won" and stage to "Closed Won".

2 Set Close Date

Enter the actual close date.

3 Update Probability

Set probability to 100%.

Losing a Deal:

1 Update Status

Change deal status to "Lost" and stage to "Closed Lost".

2 Record Loss Reason

Important: Always record why the deal was lost. This helps improve future sales.

3 Set Close Date

Enter the date the deal was lost.

2 Pipeline View

View deals organized by pipeline stage to see your sales funnel.

3 Filter & Search

Filter deals by:

  • Stage

  • Assigned owner

Probability & Win Rates

Probability indicates the likelihood of winning the deal:

  • 0-25%: Early stage, needs qualification
  • 26-50%: Qualified, proposal stage
  • 51-75%: Strong opportunity, negotiation
  • 76-99%: Very likely to close
  • 100%: Won
  • 0%: Lost
