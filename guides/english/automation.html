Overview

The CRM includes powerful automation features that work behind the scenes to help you manage leads and customers more effectively.

Lead Scoring

üìä Automatic Lead Scoring (0-100)

Leads are automatically scored based on:

  • Source quality (0-20 points)
  • BANT qualification (0-30 points)
  • Engagement activities (0-25 points)
  • Data completeness (0-15 points)
  • Authority/Priority (0-10 points)

Auto-Updates: Scores increase when leads engage with emails (+5 open, +10 click) or when activities are logged.

Duplicate Detection

üîç Real-Time Duplicate Check

Before creating a lead, the system checks for duplicates using:

  • Email: Exact match
  • Phone: 90% similarity
  • Company: 85% fuzzy matching

Result: If duplicate found, you'll see an error with duplicate lead details. You can merge or update existing lead.

Assignment Rules

üë• Automatic Lead Assignment

Leads are automatically assigned using:

  • Round-Robin: Evenly distributes leads
  • Territory-Based: Assigns by country/region
  • Load-Balanced: Assigns to least loaded SDR

Email Sequences

üìß Automatic Email Nurturing

When a lead is created with email:

  • 5-email sequence starts automatically
  • Emails scheduled: Day 0, 3, 7, 10, 14
  • Open tracking: +5 points per open
  • Click tracking: +10 points per click

Task Automation

‚úÖ Auto Task Creation

Follow-up tasks are automatically created:

  • After 7 days for new leads
  • Assigned to lead owner (SDR)
  • Prevents duplicate tasks

Health Score Calculation

üíö Customer Health Score

Automatically calculated for customers based on:

  • Activity count
  • Deal stages
  • Recency of interactions
  • Account status

Lifecycle Stage Automation

üîÑ Auto Stage Transition

Customer lifecycle stages automatically transition:

  • MQA ‚Üí SQA ‚Üí Customer ‚Üí Churned
  • Based on deals, activities, health score
