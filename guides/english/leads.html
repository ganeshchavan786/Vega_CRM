Overview

Leads are potential customers who have shown interest in your products or services. The CRM system automatically scores, assigns, and nurtures leads to help you convert them into customers.

Key Features

ü§ñ Automatic Features

  • Auto-Scoring: Leads are automatically scored (0-100) based on multiple factors
  • Auto-Assignment: Leads are automatically assigned to SDRs using round-robin or territory rules
  • Duplicate Detection: System checks for duplicates before creating leads
  • Email Sequences: Automatic email nurturing sequences start when lead is created
  • Task Creation: Follow-up tasks are automatically created after 7 days

How to Create a Lead

1 Navigate to Leads Page

Click on "Leads" in the sidebar navigation menu.

2 Click "Add Lead" Button

Click the "Add Lead" button at the top right of the leads page.

3 Fill in Lead Information

Complete the lead form with the following sections:

Basic Information:

  • First Name: Lead's first name (required)
  • Last Name: Lead's last name (required)
  • Company Name: Company name (required)
  • Email: Primary email address (required, used for duplicate check)
  • Phone: Contact phone number (required, used for duplicate check)
  • Country: Country location

Source Attribution (UTM Parameters):

  • Source: Where the lead came from (Google Ads, Website, etc.)
  • Campaign: Campaign name (e.g., CRM-Q4-2025)
  • Medium: Marketing medium (CPC, Email, Social)
  • Term: Search term or keyword

Qualification (BANT):

  • Budget Range: Available budget (e.g., ‚Çπ5-7 Lakh)
  • Authority Level: Decision Maker, Influencer, User, Gatekeeper
  • Timeline: Purchase timeline (e.g., 3-6 Months)
  • Interest Product: Product or service of interest

Privacy & Compliance:

  • GDPR Consent: Consent for data processing
  • DND Status: Do Not Disturb status
  • Opt-in Date: When lead opted in
4 System Processing

When you save the lead, the system automatically:

  • Checks for duplicates (Email + Phone + Company)
  • Calculates initial lead score (0-100)
  • Assigns lead to SDR (if not manually assigned)
  • Starts email sequence (if email provided)
‚ö†Ô∏è Duplicate Warning: If a duplicate lead is detected, you'll see an error message with details about the existing lead. You can either merge the leads or update the existing one.

Lead Scoring Explained

Leads are automatically scored from 0-100 based on multiple factors:

Factor Points Description
Source Quality 0-20 High-quality sources (referrals, events) score higher
BANT Qualification 0-30 Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline completeness
Engagement 0-25 Activities, email opens (+5), clicks (+10)
Data Completeness 0-15 How complete the lead information is
Authority/Priority 0-10 Decision maker status, priority level
üí° Tip: Lead scores automatically increase when leads engage with emails (+5 for opens, +10 for clicks) or when activities are logged.

Lead Status & Stages

Lead Status:

  • New: Just captured lead
  • Contacted: Initial contact made
  • Qualified: BANT/MEDDICC qualified
  • Unqualified: Does not meet criteria
  • Converted: Converted to Account
  • Recycled: Re-engaged after disqualification
  • Disqualified: Permanently disqualified

Lead Stage:

  • Awareness: Just aware of product
  • Consideration: Evaluating options
  • Decision: Ready to purchase
  • Converted: Converted to Account

1 Open Edit Form

2 Update Information

Modify any fields. When you update qualification fields (Budget, Authority, Timeline), the lead score will be automatically recalculated.

3 Duplicate Check

If you change email, phone, or company name, the system will re-check for duplicates.

Lead Assignment

Automatic Assignment Rules

Leads are automatically assigned using one of these methods:

  • Round-Robin: Evenly distributes leads among SDRs
  • Territory-Based: Assigns based on country/region
  • Load-Balanced: Assigns to SDR with least active leads

You can manually assign leads, or let the system auto-assign them.

Duplicate Detection

‚ö†Ô∏è How It Works: The system checks for duplicates using Email + Phone + Company combination with fuzzy matching:
  • Email: Exact match (case-insensitive)
  • Phone: 90% similarity (normalized)
  • Company: 85% similarity (fuzzy matching)

If a duplicate is found:

  • You'll see an error message with duplicate lead details
  • You can view the duplicate lead
  • You can merge duplicates if needed
  • Or update the existing lead instead

Email Sequences

Automatic Email Nurturing

When a lead is created with an email address, the system automatically:

  • Starts a 5-email sequence over 14 days
  • Schedules emails: Day 0, 3, 7, 10, 14
  • Tracks email opens (+5 points) and clicks (+10 points)
  • Updates lead score based on engagement

Conversion Eligibility

‚úÖ Conversion Trigger: A lead is eligible for conversion when:
  • Lead Score > 70
  • Lead Status = "Contacted"

Check conversion eligibility on the lead detail page or use the conversion preview feature.
